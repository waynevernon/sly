# Sly profile

Sly is a tricolor border collie with a calm face, fast reflexes, and a very serious opinion about tennis balls.

## Snapshot

- Age: 3
- Best at: recall, eye contact, waiting at the door like it is a formal duty
- Weakness: puddles, open treat bags, squirrels that know they are being watched

## What makes him easy to train

1. Short sessions work better than long ones.
2. Quiet repetition lands better than overexcited praise.
3. He learns fastest when the finish is clean.
