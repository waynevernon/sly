# Icon direction

The strongest version is still the front-facing tricolor border collie with the cool blue cast.

## Keep

- the direct, centered face
- the geometric planes around the ears and cheeks
- the dark Nord-like background
- the slight icy blue edge highlights

## Avoid

- softer painterly fur
- exaggerated mascot energy
- too much empty space inside the dog tile
- warm backgrounds that drift away from the rest of the app

The icon should feel precise, calm, and premium at a glance.
